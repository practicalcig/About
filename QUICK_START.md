# Wholeness Tracker — Quick Start Guide

## 🚀 Start in 30 Seconds

### 1. Open the File
Double-click `wholeness_tracker_enhanced.html` in your file manager, or drag it into your browser.

### 2. Create Your Account
- Enter your username (e.g., "john_doe", "sarah_2024")
- Click "Enter"
- That's it! Your account is created.

### 3. Start Logging
Pick any category (🧘 Spirit, 👨‍👩‍👧 Family, 💰 Finance, etc.):
1. Enter activity details
2. Click "Log Activity"
3. Repeat for all 7 life areas

### 4. Generate Insights
Click "Generate Report" in any section to see:
- Total activities
- This month's progress
- This week's metrics
- Actionable insights

### 5. Export Your Data
Click "📊 Export" in the header:
- Choose what to export
- Download as CSV
- Open in Excel/Google Sheets
- Analyze trends

## ❓ Common Tasks

**How do I return to my previous data?**
Simply enter the same username you used before. All data loads automatically.

**Can I track on my phone too?**
Yes! Open the HTML file on mobile with the same username.

**How do I backup my data?**
Click "📊 Export" and save the CSV file to your computer.

**What if I forget my username?**
There's no "forgot password" because there's no password. Just choose a new username, but your old data will still exist under the old username.

**Can I share this with my coach?**
Export your data as CSV and email it to them, or they can open the tracker with your permission.

**Is there a limit to how much I can log?**
No! Your browser supports millions of entries.

**What happens if I logout?**
Your data is saved. Just log back in with your username.
