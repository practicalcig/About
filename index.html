Who We Are

Practical / Team21 Academy

Practical (also operating as Team21 Academy) is a Cameroonian organization delivering intensive, bilingual (English/French) AI and digital entrepreneurship training specifically designed for youth — targeting internally displaced persons (IDPs), unemployed youth, and low-income university students across all 10 regions of Cameroon.

Our model combines technical AI skill-building with entrepreneurship frameworks and leadership development, creating pathways to both formal employment and self-employment for populations excluded from the digital economy.

Our Approach

Theory of Change

Four steps from reaching the most excluded to building a self-sustaining ecosystem of digital empowerment.

01
Reach the Unreached
Identify and recruit the most vulnerable participants through IDP camps, universities, and community networks. Provide device and internet access grants to those who cannot participate without support.
02
Build Real, Applied Skills
Deliver the four-week AI Unlocked curriculum bilingually. Train participants in AI tools, entrepreneurship (BMC, SOSTAC), leadership, and personal development. Every participant leaves with a completed Capstone project.
03
Activate Employment & Self-Employment Pathways
Connect graduates to formal employment. Recruit top performers into 2-month paid internships as junior mentors. During internship they complete ToT and launch their own Capstone — leaving with both a credential and a launched venture.
04
Scale Through a Self-Reinforcing Model
Graduates who become employed contribute to program sustainability. Alumni mentors reduce mentorship costs. Capstone showcases attract new cohorts and institutional partners. The program becomes progressively self-financing.
